## 0.0.1

* Initial release.
* Pick images from camera or gallery with `FilePickerType.camera` / `FilePickerType.gallery`.
* Pick multiple images with `SmartFilePicker.pickMultiple()`.
* Pick PDF documents with `FilePickerType.pdf`.
* Pick videos with `FilePickerType.video`.
* Optional image cropping via `SmartFilePickerConfig(enableCropping: true)`.
